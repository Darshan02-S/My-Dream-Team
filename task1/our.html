  1. BATSMEN click here 
    In cricket, batting is the act or skill of hitting the ball with a bat to score runs or prevent the loss of one's wicket.
Any player who is currently batting is denoted as a batsman, batswoman, or batter, 
regardless of whether batting is their particular area of expertise.

  2. BOWLERS click here 
    Bowling, in cricket, is the action of propelling the ball toward the wicket defended by a batter.
A player skilled at bowling is called a bowler; 
a bowler who is also a competent batter is known as an all-rounder.
